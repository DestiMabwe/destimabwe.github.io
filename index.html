About

The person behind the sprints.

I'm Destiny — a Scrum Master and aspiring Technical PM who genuinely loves both sides of the tech world. I get excited about shipping clean code just as much as I do about a well-run sprint. My BCom in Information Systems gave me the foundation; my PSM I and CAPM gave me the framework; and AI tools have become the force multiplier that lets me do the work of an entire team.

I believe the best people in tech are the ones who can talk to engineers and stakeholders in the same breath — and build something in the gaps between meetings. That's the kind of person I'm becoming.
